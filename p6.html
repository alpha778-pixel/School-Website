C.C.T.V.MONITORING SYSTEM :






The CBSE has stipulated certain conditions in the affiliations by laws to ensure safety of students in the school.



In order to cater to the safty measures the school management has decided to install a C.C.T.V.Monitoring System. This will be installed at important locations in the school. This will help monitor activities in the campus resulting in the better standards of discipline. This will also contribute towards better safety and security standards in the school.
